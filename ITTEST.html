PC Building

An Art That Takes Years to Master

Hello, on this website I will talk about the art of building a PC.

But first, let me tell you what hooked me into it.

PC building is not just putting bulky electronics into a metal box; it's the art of researching components, checking if they work together, and making sure they look good.
What do I mean by making them look good? Most PC cases have a glass panel to display the beast inside, so it needs to look the part.
You can't have an all-white setup with a black graphics card the colours need to blend in seamlessly.

what do you need to build a PC?
